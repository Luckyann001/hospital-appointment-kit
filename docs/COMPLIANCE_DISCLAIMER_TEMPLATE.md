## Patient-Facing Disclaimer

This tool provides administrative scheduling support and AI-generated pre-visit guidance for informational purposes only. It does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment, or emergency care instructions from a licensed clinician.

If you are experiencing severe symptoms (such as chest pain, breathing difficulty, stroke symptoms, severe bleeding, or risk of self-harm), call 911 or go to the nearest emergency department immediately.

By using this service, you acknowledge and consent that:

- information submitted may be used to support appointment intake workflows;
- AI output may be incomplete or incorrect and must not be treated as diagnosis;
- clinical decisions will be made by licensed healthcare professionals.
